1)you are the poem i never write,
yet read a thousand times each night.
with reality, each second i fight
when you are not by my side.

laughing softly at every sight,
yet beneath my heart lives a quiet fright—
of losing you… though you were never mine,
still, i live just to see you shine.

to trace your shadow in the light,
to hold a moment, however slight,
to live in dreams where you are near—
even if you never truly appear.

tears rolling silently.. no sound escapes 
every possibility is what my heart reshapes

i ask myself, over and over—why?
how did we fade without goodbye?

we cling on to each other, dreaming of forever,
